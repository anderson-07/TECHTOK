About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to let the authorities, government officials and even other normal members of our community to be updated and informed about the situation of our own small community's ecology, specifically the animal lives and their habitats to preserve its natural beauty and cycle.

Our Vision

Our vision is a community that is well aware of the life on land SDG, making different projects and task using this website as a tool for a better place, not only for us humans, but also for the animals who are living peacefully.

Join Us

Make the world a better place, starting from small acts like helping your local community's land life, by using this website as a tool not only for education but also for awareness that can provoke positive acts towards the ecology.
